## Engineering

I'm a versatile engineer with experience across software engineering, infrastructure, mobile, web, and operations. I've led teams, driven platform migrations, reduced latency, modernized infrastructure, and stayed hands-on when needed.

Strong fundamentals matter more than fashionable tools. That belief shapes both how I build teams and how I solve technical problems.

## Community work

I've dedicated significant time to social empowerment and community building.

From 2014 to 2024, I contributed to IAmTalentedSG, used technology to improve operational efficiency, and served as the event photographer. The initiative focused on uplifting youths in at-risk communities.

During the COVID-19 crisis, I co-founded Project Stable Staples with a small team responding to the needs of families living in rental communities. I launched the donation portal in under two days, shaped donor engagement and public communications, and helped drive a campaign that raised more than SGD 160,000. That support reached more than 2,600 individuals across more than 600 households.

After the peak pandemic period, the work continued at a smaller and more sustainable scale so families in transition still had a rapid support system until existing social infrastructure could take over.

## Philanthropy and mentoring

Outside direct on-the-ground work, I support philanthropic efforts such as the Lai Yu Hua Bursary. I do not just fund support. I stay engaged with recipients and work to make that support meaningful.

I've spent years contributing to broader communities, from GeekcampSG and NUSHackers in my student years to mentoring and alumni engagement through the National University of Singapore.

## How I work

I work from first principles. I value resilient teams, practical execution, and using whatever skills are most useful in the moment. In community work, that can mean logistics, communications, fundraising, or building tools quickly. In engineering, it means combining leadership with enough technical depth to stay credible and effective.
