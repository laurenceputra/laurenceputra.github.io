I work where execution, complexity, and judgment meet. My focus is helping teams and leaders make systems simpler, more resilient, and easier to govern.

The goal is not more activity. It is better structure, clearer decisions, and durable leverage.

That through-line shows up in engineering leadership, operational efficiency, and community work. Today, I lead Operational Support and Efficiency at TikTok, where my team uses AI to improve how tech operations run and explores what AI-native operations can become in practice.

The work itself changes, but the principle does not: understand the real constraint, remove unnecessary complexity, and leave enough room for judgment to do its job.

**为无为，则无不治.** The goal is not constant intervention. It is to build systems that can carry themselves.
