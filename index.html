About Me

Engineering leader with a passion for technology and social change

I'm currently the Site Leader for Server Architecture at TikTok Singapore, where I lead teams building highly scalable, secure, and compliant infrastructure serving billions of users worldwide. My work focuses on microservices evolution, global multi-data center operations, and ensuring high availability for one of the world's fastest-growing platforms.

With over a decade of experience in engineering leadership, I've built and scaled teams at PayPal, SP Group, Nugit, and 99.co. I hold a degree in Computer Science from the National University of Singapore (2013) and have deep expertise in distributed systems, security, and site reliability engineering.

Beyond technology, I'm deeply committed to social impact and community empowerment. I co-founded Project Stable Staples during COVID-19, raising over SGD 160,000 to support 600 families. I'm also a founding member of IAmTalented.SG, empowering at-risk youth through skills-based education since 2014.
