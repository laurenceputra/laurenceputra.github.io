Tech Leadership & Innovation

As a technology leader, I bring together deep technical expertise with strategic vision to build teams and systems that create lasting impact. My journey spans from hands-on engineering to leading organizations in scaling their technology and people.

Throughout my career at PayPal, 99.co, Nugit, and SP Group, I've led engineering initiatives in distributed systems, security, and platform architecture. I've managed teams building mobile applications serving millions, architected backend systems for fintech platforms, and directed digital infrastructure transformations. My leadership philosophy centers on empowering engineers to do their best work while maintaining technical excellence.

With an academic background spanning Computer Science and Liberal Arts, I approach technology leadership with both technical rigor and human-centered thinking. I believe the best technology solutions come from diverse teams working on problems that matter, which is why I'm passionate about creating inclusive engineering cultures and mentoring the next generation of technical leaders.

Philanthropy & Social Impact

My commitment to social impact drives much of my work outside of technology. I believe in creating opportunities for those who need them most and building systems that support sustainable community development.

IAmTalented.SG

As a core team member of IAmTalented.SG, I work to empower underserved youth through skills-based education and mentorship. IAT reaches out to youths at Secondary 2 and 3 levels, providing opportunities to explore non-academic pathways through hands-on workshops. This program benefits youths without access to such resources within their schools or family environment.

Project Stable Staples

I co-founded Project Stable Staples to address food security for lower-income families in Singapore. What started as a response to COVID-19 has evolved into a sustained effort to ensure families have consistent access to essential supplies during difficult times.

Collaborate.SG

Through Collaborate.SG, I work to connect and coordinate social sector initiatives, helping organizations amplify their impact through collaboration and shared resources.

My philosophy: True leadership means using privilege, expertise, and resources to create lasting positive change. Technology and philanthropy are not separate pursuits—they're complementary tools for building a more equitable society.

Community Building & Leadership

Community building has been central to my career. From organizing Singapore's largest tech unconference to creating inclusive spaces for developers and designers, I've worked to strengthen the bonds that make our tech ecosystem thrive.

I led GeekcampSG from 2012 to 2014, growing the community from 200 participants to over 1,300, with a third being students. This platform provided a space for knowledge sharing, networking, and inspiration for Singapore's tech community.

At PayPal Singapore, I built a developer community engagement program from the ground up. Starting with a team of 2, we grew to over 20 PayPal engineers hosting up to 12 community meetups per month, creating a welcoming space for diverse groups including UXSG, PyLadies, and many others.

I've also been privileged to share insights through speaking engagements, including panel discussions with industry leaders from Google and Pivotal Labs, and technical conferences on topics like Continuous Delivery and DevOps.
