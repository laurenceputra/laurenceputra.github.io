Channel 8 Frontline Interview on Project Stable Staples

I was featured on Channel 8's Frontline current affairs programme to discuss Project Stable Staples (PSS), our community-driven initiative to support families in need during the COVID-19 crisis.

The interview covered the background of Project Stable Staples, what we had accomplished prior to the interview, and where we aimed to go in the future. Project Stable Staples was co-founded to provide rapid support to families in rental communities, raising over SGD 160,000 to support 600+ families.

Our work was recognized with the NVPC PVPA People of Good award presented by President Halimah Yacob. The initiative continues beyond the pandemic, including the Lai Yu Hua Bursary and ongoing support for vulnerable communities.

The interview discussed both past achievements and ongoing strategies, showcasing our commitment to ensuring families in transition received rapid support until they could be helped by existing social infrastructure. The overarching narrative is one of sustained commitment to social empowerment and rapid, community-focused support systems.
