People of Good Award

I am deeply honored to receive the NVPC PVPA People of Good award from President Halimah Yacob for my work with Project Stable Staples. This recognition reflects not just individual effort, but the collective power of community coming together to support those in need.

Project Stable Staples was co-founded during the COVID-19 crisis to address food security for lower-income families in Singapore. We successfully raised over SGD 160,000 and supported 600+ families in rental communities with essential supplies, job search assistance, and long-term support programs.

As an engineering leader passionate about solving social problems, my work spans everything from on-the-ground support for at-risk communities to broader philanthropic initiatives. The initiative provided rapid support to families in transition until public social infrastructure could take over, and continues beyond the pandemic through programs like the Lai Yu Hua Bursary.

For over a decade, I've also contributed to IAmTalentedSG, enhancing operational efficiency and serving as an event photographer for at-risk youth events. Now, I focus on sustainable philanthropic work, actively engaging with beneficiaries to make a meaningful difference.

Currently serving as Site Leader for the Server Architecture org at TikTok Singapore, I manage the eSRE function while balancing technical expertise with people management. My engineering background spans software, mobile development, and data center management, with side projects continuing to support both work and charitable initiatives.

This award represents a journey from direct community engagement to sustainable philanthropic work, and a continued drive to make positive impact in society. It belongs to everyone who contributed to making Project Stable Staples a success.
